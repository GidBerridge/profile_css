About

Who am I?

A London (UK) based web developer open to working locally or remotely. I’ve always been drawn to where technology and creativity collide which is why I am passionate about web development.

Where did I come from?

I started off in the music industry working as a musician, promoter and sound engineer. Later on I worked as a video editor and AV technician on corporate events.

My journey into tech

I’ve always had a passion for learning and technology. At university I studied an MA in Creative Sound and Media Technology and made a few Wordpress sites to promote music projects. Delving into the maker culture, I taught myself Python and C++ whilst making projects using Arduino’s and Raspberry Pi’s. After studying an IoT course, I became more interested in the potential of web apps so enrolled on a web developer bootcamp at Le Wagon and decided to make a career change. I have been freelancing as a web developer since succesfully completing the bootcamp in September 2021.
